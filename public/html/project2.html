Description

This project explores the intersection of music, motion, and generative art by transforming the webcam into a digital conducting tool. Using p5.js and p5.sound, I designed a system where real‑time motion captured by the camera controls both animated visuals and layered audio voices. Each animated object on the canvas represents a “singer” in a virtual choir. As the conductor moves, gestures are translated into musical parameters such as pitch, volume, and timbre, while simultaneously influencing the size, color, and behavior of the visual elements.
